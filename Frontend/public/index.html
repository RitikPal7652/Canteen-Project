📋 About Placement Cell

The Training and Placement Cell (T&P) at Government Engineering College serves as a bridge between students and the corporate world. We strive to provide excellent placement opportunities and prepare students for successful careers.

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to bring the best companies to campus and ensure students are well-prepared for recruitment processes. We provide training in aptitude, technical skills, and soft skills to enhance employability.
